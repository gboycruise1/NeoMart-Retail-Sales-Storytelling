# 🛒 NeoMart Retail Sales Storytelling Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes NeoMart's retail sales data to uncover why sales increased while profitability declined. Using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn, the analysis explores sales trends, profitability, regional performance, customer satisfaction, delivery efficiency, and the impact of discounts.

The project combines data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), business visualization, dashboard development, and executive storytelling to support management decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions

This analysis answers the following questions:

- Has sales performance improved over time?
- Is profit growing at the same rate as sales?
- Which regions perform best and worst?
- Which product categories are the most profitable?
- How do discounts affect profitability?
- What factors impact customer satisfaction?
- What recommendations should management implement?

## 📈 Key Insights

### Sales
- Sales increased steadily throughout the year, indicating growing customer demand.

### Profit
- Profit did not increase at the same pace as sales, suggesting margin erosion.

### Discounts
- Higher discounts were associated with lower profitability.

### Regional Performance
- Some regions consistently outperformed others in both sales and customer satisfaction.

### Customer Experience
- Longer delivery times were linked with lower customer satisfaction.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Reduce excessive discounting.
- Improve delivery operations in underperforming regions.
- Increase marketing for high-profit product categories.
- Review pricing strategies for low-margin products.
- Monitor KPIs using an executive dashboard.
